Gaze-based Control for Assistive Arms

Zhongchun Yu, Shivani Guptasarma

project page

This project explores simplifying the control of assistive robotic arms, like the Jaco arm, by integrating gaze tracking through an AR headset (Hololens2) and surface electromyography (EMG) inputs to enable users with upper-limb amputations to perform complex tasks more intuitively.
