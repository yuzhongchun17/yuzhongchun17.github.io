Hello!

I am an Mechanical Engineering (Robotics) MS student at Stanford University. I am currently working on a Neruosurgical Robot System project in the Collaborative Haptics and Robotics in Medicine Lab advised by Prof. Allison Okamura. My research interests are diverse within the field of robotics, encompassing robot manipulation, medical robotics, and robot perception.

Prior to Stanford, I completed my undergraduate degree in Mechanical Engineering with a minor in Computer Science from UIUC. During my undergrad, I worked on a portable measurement device which collect quantitative biomechanical data from users in Human Dynamics and Controls Lab advised by Prof. Elizabeth T Hsiao-Wecksler. I was also part of the Advanced Controls Reserach Laboratory where I was working on the perception track when building up an autonomous quadrotor under the guidance of Minkyung Kim and Sheng Cheng.
